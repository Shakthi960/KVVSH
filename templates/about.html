Building Global Businesses Through Innovation

KVVSH Group of Companies Pvt Ltd is a diversified multinational conglomerate headquartered in Nagore, Tamil Nadu, India. Since our establishment in 2017, we have expanded across multiple industries including technology, media, healthcare, education, infrastructure, manufacturing, transportation and digital services.

Our mission is to create sustainable businesses that positively impact society while delivering exceptional value to customers, employees and stakeholders worldwide.
